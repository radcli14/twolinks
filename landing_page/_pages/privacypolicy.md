# Privacy Policy for TwoLinks
Effective Date: May 18, 2026

DCDC LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the TwoLinks mobile and web application (the "App") available on iOS, Android, and the web. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our App.

<br>

## 1. Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. TwoLinks operates entirely on your device. No account creation, login, or network connection to our servers is required or performed.

<br>

## 2. Camera Access
On iOS and Android, the App may request access to your device's camera solely to enable the Augmented Reality (AR) feature. Camera data is used only to display the live camera feed within the App and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted off your device.

<br>

## 3. Third-Party Services
TwoLinks does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

<br>

## 4. Children's Privacy
Because we collect no personal information, TwoLinks is safe for users of all ages.
